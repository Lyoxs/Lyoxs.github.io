about me

This is me

So... you made it to the about me section. I just wanted to say thank you for visiting my site and I hope you enjoy my music.

But now let me tell you a little bit about me.

My name is Maik, and I love making music and stuff. I started playing guitar at the age of 6, and I loved it from the beginning.

A few years ago, I started to make music. I was always fascinated by the idea of creating something that can make people feel something. Eventually, I ended up in FL Studio :D After a while, I discovered drum and bass, and I fell in love with the genre.

I am constantly exploring new sounds and experimenting with different styles to create songs I love.

Music is my way of expressing myself and connecting with others. I hope that my music brings you joy and happiness. Thank you for visiting my site and supporting my musical journey. Stay tuned for more exciting releases!
